# Prompt cau hinh Context (Quan trong)

Sau moi ngay, doc noi dung lam viec va luu vao Context de su dung cho cac lan sau.

Nguon du lieu uu tien:
1) `.automation/context-latest.json`
2) cac ban ghi trong `.automation/context-history.jsonl` (moi dong la 1 JSON)
3) ghi chu thu cong tu `.automation/daily-notes.txt`

Yeu cau:
- Tom tat nhung viec da lam, van de gap phai, va ke hoach tiep theo.
- Su dung context moi nhat truoc, chi quay lai lich su khi can.
- Khong luu thong tin nhay cam (mat khau, token, api key) vao notes/context.

Tuy chon nang cao:
- Co the dong bo sang MongoDB hoac Supabase neu can truy van tu nhieu may.
